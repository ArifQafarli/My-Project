Our Services

“Inland railway transportation” CJSC

We have extensive experience, well-established railway transportation schemes for any routes in Azerbaijan. This enables us to make transportation as comfortable, reliable and efficient as possible.

“Import deliveries”

Client support at all stages of shipping 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year!

“Export deliveries”

We provide the wide spectrum and high quality railway transportation services.

“Transit deliveries”

Affordable pricing of transit railway rates is what we constantly strive for. We value our customers, so they have an opportunity to get freight rates with discounts. Promos depend on your orders & routes.

“Terminal and port handling”

As a process port terminal handling of cargoes comprises a variety of operations.We are offering handling services suitable for every type of cargo - from containerised general cargo to bulk cargo, from project and plant to liquids.
